Ad Copy Optimization Tips

  • Use strong, action-oriented language in your headlines
  • Include your main keyword in both the headline and description
  • Highlight unique selling points and benefits
  • Create a sense of urgency with time-limited offers
  • Use numbers and statistics to increase credibility
  • Tailor your message to your target audience
  • Keep your copy concise and to the point
  • Use power words that evoke emotion
