Introduction to SEO

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the practice of improving your website to increase its visibility for relevant searches. The better visibility your pages have in search results, the more likely you are to garner attention and attract prospective and existing customers to your business.

On-Page SEO

On-page SEO refers to the practice of optimizing individual web pages in order to rank higher and earn more relevant traffic in search engines. It involves both the content and HTML source code of a page that can be optimized.

On-Page SEO Checklist

  • Optimize your title tags and meta descriptions
  • Use header tags (H1, H2, H3) correctly
  • Optimize your content for target keywords
  • Improve your URL structure
  • Optimize images (including alt tags)
  • Improve internal linking
  • Ensure your content is high-quality and valuable

Off-Page SEO

Off-page SEO refers to actions taken outside of your own website to impact your rankings within search engine results pages (SERPs). This primarily involves improving search engine and user perception of a site's popularity, relevance, trustworthiness, and authority.

Off-Page SEO Strategies

  • Build high-quality backlinks
  • Engage in social media marketing
  • Create and optimize business listings
  • Encourage brand mentions
  • Engage in influencer marketing
  • Participate in forums and online communities

Technical SEO

Technical SEO refers to improving the technical aspects of a website to increase the ranking of its pages in search engines. It makes a website easier to crawl and more comprehensible for search engines.

Technical SEO Checklist

  • Improve site speed and performance
  • Ensure your site is mobile-friendly
  • Implement SSL security (HTTPS)
  • Create and optimize your XML sitemap
  • Fix broken links and improve site structure
  • Implement structured data markup
  • Optimize your robots.txt file

Local SEO

Local SEO is the practice of optimizing a business's online presence to attract more business from relevant local searches. These searches take place on Google and other search engines.

Local SEO Best Practices

  • Claim and optimize your Google My Business listing
  • Ensure NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency across the web
  • Get listed in online business directories
  • Encourage and manage customer reviews
  • Create local content
  • Optimize for voice search and mobile
