How to Set Up an App Password for Gmail

Note: App passwords are required for our Email Automation Tool to securely access your Gmail account. This guide will walk you through the process of creating an app password.

Steps to Create an App Password:

  1. Go to your Google Account.
  2. Select Security.
  3. Under "Signing in to Google," select 2-Step Verification.
  4. At the bottom of the page, select App passwords.
  5. Enter a name that helps you remember where you'll use the app password.
  6. Select Generate.
  7. To enter the app password on your device, follow the instructions shown. The app password is the 16-character code that generates on your device.
  8. Select Done.

Important: If you don't see the option to create an app password, it might be because:

  • 2-Step Verification is not set up for your account
  • 2-Step Verification is set up for security keys only
  • Your account is through work, school, or other organization
  • You've turned on Advanced Protection for your account

Using Your App Password

Once you've generated your app password, you can use it in place of your regular password when setting up our Email Automation Tool. This provides a secure way for our application to access your Gmail account without compromising your main account password.
