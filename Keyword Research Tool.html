Keyword Research Tips

  • Focus on long-tail keywords for less competition and higher conversion rates.
  • Consider the search intent behind keywords (informational, navigational, transactional).
  • Use a mix of broad and specific keywords in your content strategy.
  • Analyze keyword difficulty and search volume to prioritize your efforts.
  • Keep an eye on trending topics and seasonal keywords in your industry.
