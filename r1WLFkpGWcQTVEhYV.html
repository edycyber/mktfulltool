Ad Performance Case Studies

Case Study 1: E-commerce Success with Google Ads

An online retailer increased their ROI by 150% using targeted Google Shopping campaigns and search ads. Key strategies included:

  • Optimizing product feed for Google Merchant Center
  • Implementing negative keywords to reduce wasted spend
  • Creating ad groups based on product categories
  • Utilizing responsive search ads for improved performance

Case Study 2: B2B Lead Generation with LinkedIn Ads

A SaaS company reduced their cost per lead by 40% using LinkedIn's targeting capabilities. Their approach included:

  • Leveraging job title and company size targeting
  • Creating content-rich sponsored updates
  • Implementing lead gen forms for easy conversions
  • A/B testing ad copy and visuals for optimal performance
